
16. **Scenario 16: Profession Traits Column & Lock**
    - Select an occupation with unique traits (e.g., Veteran for 'Desensitized' or Lumberjack for 'Axe Man').
    - Verify:
      - The trait appears at the very top of the **Positive Traits** column.
      - The trait has a **Padlock icon** instead of a number at the end of the row.
      - The trait is unclickable and dimmed (Locked).
